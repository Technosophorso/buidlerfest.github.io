Bringing Cardano to Asia: Why Vietnam for Builder Fest

In 2024, the first Cardano Builder Fest united builders to explore challenges, share ideas, and work together on solutions that push the boundaries of what's possible on Cardano.

In 2025, Cardano Builder Fest #2, taking what we have learned and experienced, we're expanding to Asia with more ideas, and a stronger, united community.

We have considered where would be an ideal location such as Singapore, Thailand, Hong Kong or Japan. Asia offers vibrant diversity. After much consideration, we have landed on Vietnam to be the ideal choice to the next Buidler Fest!

Here are some of the various reasons:

Growing Tech scene

Vietnam is a young country and it's a key hub for skill developers in the region. There is a vibrant tech scene and startup ecosystem. As an event targeting developers, Vietnam is the ideal choice location to grow the Cardano community.

Costs and affordability

Vietnam offers accessible and cost-effective options for planning a cost-effective conference for Asia based developers and international travelers.

Cultural Experience

Vietnam offers a rich and diverse cultural experience, with stunning natural landscapes, vibrant cities, and mouthwatering food!

Overcoming Challenges

We embarked on planning for Buidler Fest 2025 since October 2024 and had faced significant challenges. Notably, countries in the region are still establishing regulations and framework with regards to blockchain1. Therefore, the planning team had to work closely with the government to issue the license necessary for Buidler Fest 2025.

With the introduction of National Blockchain Strategy by Vietnam government in late Oct 20242, we believe that by working closely with the Vietnamese government for Cardano Buidler Fest would benefit the ecosystem in the long run.

Therefore after months of preparation, we are proud share that we have officially received the licence to run Buidler Fest #2 in Vietnam, Danang. We look forward to seeing you there!

1 Vietnam's Blockchain and Crypto Industry Overview

Currently, Vietnam does not have a specific and comprehensive legal framework to regulate the crypto industry. However, there are indications that the Vietnamese government has begun researching and reviewing regulations in this field, including management, taxation, and monitoring of crypto-related activities to ensure transparency and accountability.

Here are some notable updates to date:

  • 2017: Cryptocurrency is not recognized as a legal payment method, and the use of crypto for payments is prohibited.
  • 2018: The Vietnamese government imposes penalties for using crypto in payment transactions.
  • 2019: The prohibition on the use of crypto in individual and organizational transactions is further strengthened.
  • 2020: Crypto trading and ownership continue to grow in Vietnam, though there are no laws regarding crypto ownership. The Ministry of Education and Training partners with Viction (formerly TomoChain) to digitize and store national diplomas.
  • 2021: Prime Minister Pham Minh Chinh calls for research on a legal framework for crypto.
  • 2022: The State Bank of Vietnam begins researching and testing central bank digital currencies (CBDCs) and enhances discussions on a legal framework for crypto and anti-money laundering laws.
  • 2024: National Strategy for Blockchain Application and Development up to 2025, with a vision to 2030. The strategy aims for Vietnam to join the leading nations in the region and secure an international position in the blockchain industry by 2030.

International Crypto Market Compared to Vietnam

Many countries worldwide have regulations on blockchain and crypto market. Countries like the United States, Canada, Singapore, Japan, South Korea, the United Kingdom, France, and most European nations recognize crypto as a legal asset. Conversely, countries such as China, Bangladesh, Pakistan, and some others currently ban crypto. Vietnam takes a middle-ground approach, prohibiting the use of crypto as a payment method but allowing ownership rights.

2 National Strategy for Blockchain Application and Development

Overview

The National Strategy for Blockchain Application and Development was issued on 22 October 2024, highlighting Vietnam's acknowledgment of blockchain as a crucial technology in the digital era. These strategies present ambitious goals and comprehensive action plans to harness transformative technologies for national advancement and modernization.

Key goals and objectives

  • By 2025: Vietnam aims to establish a robust blockchain ecosystem, including a national blockchain infrastructure, pilot testbeds, support interoperability, sharing between blockchains and promote the integration of blockchain into key sectors like finance, healthcare, and education.
  • By 2030: The nation seeks to expand its blockchain infrastructure, gain international recognition in blockchain development and foster homegrown blockchain brands.

Action plan

The strategy sets out a phased approach to blockchain adoption, including five key tasks:

  • Develop a legal framework for blockchain application and development
  • Establish blockchain infrastructure and a blockchain industry ecosystem
  • Cultivate a skilled blockchain workforce
  • Promote blockchain adoption in various sectors
  • Encourage research, innovation and international collaboration

This strategy lays the foundation for sustainable blockchain development in Vietnam, contributing to social and economic growth and building a transparent, secure digital economy.
