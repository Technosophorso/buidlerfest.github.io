Cardano Builder Fest Asia: An Update on Our Journey and How You Can Get Involved

Building Together, One Step at a Time

As we gear up for Cardano Builder Fest Asia, we are thrilled to bring the global Cardano community together to collaborate, innovate, and share knowledge. However, organizing an event of this scale comes with its unique challenges, and we want to share our progress and plans with you.

Pre-Registration Is Now Open!

To make Cardano Builder Fest Asia as dynamic and engaging as possible, we are inviting you to share your ideas and interests through our pre-registration form. This is your chance to shape the event agenda and contribute to the conversations that matter most to you.

Details of the event:

  • Date: April or May 2025 (TBD)
  • Location: Vietnam

To sign up for pre-registration:

  • If you're interested in becoming a speaker, fill out the form.
  • If you'd like to participate, fill out the form.

Important Note: The pre-registration forms are intended to gather insights on topics of interest and estimate the number of attendees. Submitting the forms does not guarantee a speaker or participant slot.

Important Travel Advisory

We know many of you are eager to make travel arrangements. However, we kindly request that you DO NOT book flights or accommodations until we officially confirm the venue and dates for the event. (see Navigating Local Regulations below).

Your patience and understanding mean the world to us as we navigate these regulatory steps to ensure a successful and compliant event.

Interested? Here's what you can do

  • Complete the Pre-Registration Form: Let us know your interests, ideas, and any topics you'd like to see at the event.
  • Spread the Word: Share this update with fellow Cardano developers and community members who might be interested.
  • Stay Connected: Follow our announcements (on twitter @buidler_fest) to stay updated on the licensing process, early bird tickets, and other event details etc.

Navigating Local Regulations

To host this event in compliance with local laws, we are in the process of applying for the necessary licenses from the government. This process requires us to submit additional information about the event, our speakers and organizing team as part of the application.
