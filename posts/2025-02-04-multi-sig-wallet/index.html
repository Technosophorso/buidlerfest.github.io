Managing Funds and Multi-sig Wallet - Transparency and Governance

When organizing an event or working on an open-source project, managing funds isn't just about balancing a budget, it's about building trust, fostering accountability, and ensuring that every decision supports the larger vision. In projects like Cardano Buidler Fest, where collaboration and transparency are at the heart of everything we do, fund management becomes particularly critical.

A Practical Tool for Team Collaboration

The Multisig Platform allows multiple team members to participate in every financial decision. Each transaction requires approval from 3 out of 4 parties involved, ensuring collective agreement before any funds are spent.

Convenience in fund management

When a team member needs to request the use of funds, they can create a new transaction through the web interface. The transaction will only be executed once required parties sign for approval.

Collective decision making

Decisions on spending are reviewed and approved collectively by the team. This ensures that every allocation is deliberate, purposeful, and aligned with the event's goals.

Accountability through consensus

Multisig empowers the team to share responsibility for fund management, eliminating individual control and promoting accountability at every step.

Easy to facilitate refunds

Any invalid transactions can be easily refunded by the team. It a click of a button, the transaction is created and to be signed by the required parties.
